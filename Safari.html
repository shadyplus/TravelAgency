ATV safari

Tour days

We organize the tour daily from 08:30 to 12:00 and from 15:00 to 18:00.

Program

  • In the morning or in the afternoon you leave Hurghada for the desert base where you transfer to ATVs and drive to the Bedouin village.
  • In the village, you ride camels and taste Bedouin tea.
  • Then you return to the base and have dinner.
  • After 3 hours you return to Hurghada.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • An English-speaking guide-instructor works on the excursion.
  • The price includes ATV rides, camel rides, and Bedouin tea tasting.

Price

Adults and children from 16 years pay $20 per person, children under 16 years are not allowed to ride.

For individual travel, there is an additional fee of $20 per group.
