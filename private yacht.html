Private yacht

Sea voyage on a private yacht

Tour days

We organize a tour every day from 08:30 to 16:00.

Program

  • In the morning, sail from Hurghada. Stop at the coral reef for snorkeling and swimming. After sailing, have lunch and fish from the yacht.
  • Back to the hotel at 16:00.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • At sea give out equipment for snorkeling and fishing.
  • The price includes lunch and soft drinks.

What is not included in the price

  • Price does not include visits to the islands — optional $10 per person.
  • Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

Price

Prices are different and depends on how many person will be ob yacht

Up to 6 adults $150 per yacht, optional island +$10 per person

Up to 8 adults $250 per yacht, optional island +$10 per person

Up to 9 adults $220 per yacht, optional island +$10 per person

Up to 12 adults $330 per yacht, optional island +$10 per person

Up to 9 adults $190 per yacht, with a see-through bottom, no island.

Yachts are booked on 30% prepayment — refundable in case of cancellation up to 24 hours before the trip.
