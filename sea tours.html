Paradise Island

Tour to Paradise Island from Hurghada

Tour days

We organize a tour every day from 08:30 to 17:00.

Program

  • In the morning you sail from Hurghada. Make stops in the open sea, where you swim with a snorkel and flippers on the coral reef, as well as ride on an inflatable banana and a pillbox.
  • Then you land on Paradise Island for 2 hours for rest and swimming from the shore.
  • After resting on the island you return to the boat for lunch.
  • At 17:00 you return to Hurghada.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • The price includes the use of sun loungers and umbrellas on Paradise Island.
  • In the sea give equipment for snorkeling — mask, flippers, snorkel and life vest.
  • An experienced snorkeling guide works on the yacht.
  • The price includes lunch, non-alcoholic drinks on the yacht, and a ride on an inflatable banana and a boat.

What is not included in the price

Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

Price

Adults pay $28 per person, children from 6 to 11 years old include $17, children under 6 years old are free.

Sahl Hasheesh 7-in-1: Horseback riding, Pharaohs Gate, sunken city, bathyscaphe, fishing, snorkeling, colored fountains

Tour days

We organize a tour on Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday from 09:00 to 20:00.

Program

  • In the morning you arrive in the desert and ride horses.
  • After the ride, you stop at the Pharaoh,s Gate at the entrance to Sahl Hasheesh.
  • Then come to the seafront, relax on the beach near the sunken city and swim on the coral reef.
  • At 14:00 you set sail on the yacht «Batiskaft», the bottom of which is 4 meters underwater, and through the large panoramic windows, you observe the sea inhabitants of the Red Sea.
  • At 15:00, sail the yacht into the sea and make a stop on the coral reef for snorkeling and swimming. After sailing, during sunset, fish from the yacht.
  • After fishing, you have dinner on the boat and return to the waterfront in Sahl Hasheesh.
  • Then you visit Fountain Square and drive the electric cars, with a driver, along the evening promenade.
  • At 20:00 you return to Hurghada.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • At sea give out equipment for snorkeling and fishing.
  • The price includes light snacks on the beach, a seafood dinner, and non-alcoholic drinks.

Price
