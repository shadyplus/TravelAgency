Jungle Aquapark

Jungle Aquapark in Hurghada

Tour days

We organize a tour every day from 09:30 to 16:30.

Program

  • In the morning you go to Jungle Aquapark in Hurghada. In the Aquapark you ride 35 water rides, 21 of which are for adults and 14 for children.
  • During your visit to the Aquapark, you have lunch at the restaurant.
  • At 17:00 you return to the hotel.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.

  • The price includes tickets to the water park, lunch, and soft drinks.

What is not included in the price

  • Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

Price

Adults pay $35 per person, children from 3 to 11 years old include $20.

Parasailing

Parasailing in Hurghada

Tour days

We organize a tour every day.

Program

  • In the open sea, a bird's-eye view, fly on a parachute towed by a boat.
  • You fly a parachute alone or with a partner.
  • No special skills are needed.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.

  • Parachute flight up to 7 minutes.

What is not included in the price

  • Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

Price

One person pays $20, if you ride together $30 for two.

Two skydivers can ride together only if the total weight of the skydivers is up to 150 kg.

Museum of Antiquities in Hurghada

Tour to the Museum of Egyptian Antiquities in Hurghada

Tour days

We organize a tour every day — museum hours are from 10:00 to 14:00 and from 17:00 to 23:00.

Program

  • In the morning or afternoon, you go to the Museum of Antiquities in Hurghada.

  • In the museum English-speaking guide leads a tour for 1.5 hours.

  • After the tour you can see the exhibits on your own and take pictures — there are 1791 exhibits and 4 mummies in the museum.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.

  • There is an English-speaking guide on the tour.

  • The price includes entrance tickets to the museum and 1.5 hours guided tour

What is not included in the price

  • Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

Price

Adults pay $25 per person, children ages 6 to 11 inclusive $15.

Reservations are possible from 2 adults.
