El Gouna

Egyptian Venice El Gouna

Tour days

We organize a tour every day from 08:30 to 16:30.

Program

  • In the morning you sail from Hurghada. Make a stop in the open sea near the dolphin reef, where you swim with a snorkel and flippers.
  • Then on a yacht, the bottom of which is 4 meters underwater, go to the coral reef and through large panoramic windows observe the sea inhabitants of the Red Sea.
  • Then take a boat ride through the water channels of the resort town of El Gouna, whose second name is the Egyptian Venice.
  • After a tour of El Gouna, you return to the boat for lunch. Those interested catch fish from the boat.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • In the sea give equipment for snorkeling — mask, flippers, snorkel and life vest.
  • An experienced snorkeling guide works on the yacht.
  • The price includes lunch and soft drinks.

What is not included in the price

  • Price does not include drinks and a walk on the Nile — optional $10.

  • Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

Price

Adults pay $35 per person, children from 6 to 11 years old include $20, children under 6 years old are free.
