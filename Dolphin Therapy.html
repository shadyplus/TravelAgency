Dolphin Therapy

Dolphin therapy — VIP swimming with a dolphin

Tour days

We organize swimming every day except Saturday at 08:00, 08:30, 09:00, and 09:30.

Program

  • In the morning you will take an individual transfer to the dolphinarium.

  • At the dolphinarium, you get acquainted with the dolphin therapist and trainer, choose a wetsuit, and pass the initial instruction.

  • Then for 25-30 minutes, you swim with the dolphin in the pool accompanied by the trainer and the dolphin therapist.

  • After the swim, you return to the hotel.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the swim.

  • The price includes a session with a dolphin therapist, dolphin, and dolphin trainer lasting 25-30 minutes.

  • Wetsuit and towel issued on the spot.

  • You are allowed to take photos and videos while swimming.

  • Up to 3 chaperones are included.

Price

Adults pay $195, children $185.

Dolphin therapy reservations are subject to a $30 prepayment — prepayment is refundable upon cancellation no later than 24 hours before the trip.
