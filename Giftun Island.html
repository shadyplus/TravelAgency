Giftun Island

Tour to Giftune Paradise Island

Tour days

We organize a tour every day from 08:30 to 17:00.

Program

  • In the morning you sail from Hurghada. Make two stops in the open sea, where you swim with a snorkel and flippers on the coral reef.
  • Then land by boat on the island Giftun, for rest and swimming from the shore.
  • Then on a boat with a transparent bottom landed on the island of Utopia, for recreation and swimming from the shore.
  • After resting on the island, you swim back to the yacht and have lunch.
  • At 17:00 you return to Hurghada.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • In the sea give equipment for snorkeling — mask, flippers, snorkel and life vest.
  • An experienced snorkeling guide works on the yacht.
  • The price includes lunch and soft drinks.

What is not included in the price

  • Separately pay for transfers from the outlying areas of El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, and Makadi Bay — $5 per person, from 2 people or $25 per minibus both ways.

  • Paradise Island VIP: Improved food, extra ride on the inflatable banana and tabby — $22 per person, children from 5 to 11 years inclusive $13.
