Dolphinarium

Dolphinarium in Hurghada

Tour days

We organize a tour every day except Saturday from 14:00 to 17:00.

Program

  • At 14:00 you go to the dolphinarium in Hurghada and watch the show with the dolphins.
  • If you want after the show, for a fee, swim with the dolphins.

What is included in the price

  • Transfer picks up from the hotel in Hurghada and brings back after the tour.
  • The price includes admission tickets to the show program.

Transfer:

    1- from Hurghada : 5$ for one person for both ways

    2- from Safaga : 10$ for one person for both ways

  • The price does not include a swim with the dolphins, optional — 10 minutes $65.

  • When you swim with dolphins, you can not take photos and videos — a photo session with dolphins is $8 for 1 photo or $ 40 for the disk. Photo session with dolphins after the show without swimming $30.

  • Dolphin feeding at 11:00 and 15:00.

  • Price

  • Adults pay $15 per person, children from 5 to 11 inclusive $10, children under 5 free of charge.
